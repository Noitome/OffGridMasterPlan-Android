### Backlink Quality Metrics

#### Domain Authority Targets
- Tier 1: DA 80+ (major publications, government sites)
- Tier 2: DA 50-79 (industry publications, reputable blogs)
- Tier 3: DA 30-49 (niche sites, local businesses)
- Tier 4: DA 10-29 (supplementary, long-tail opportunities)

#### Relevance Scoring
- 10/10: Direct renewable energy/off-grid living
- 8/10: Sustainability/environmental
- 6/10: Home improvement/construction
- 4/10: General lifestyle/finance
- 2/10: Generic directories
